Miller Storm Core Values & Mission

Video Summary


This video introduces the mission and core values of Miller Storm. It establishes the company as a personal development-driven organization that prioritizes growth, accountability, integrity, and putting people first in both customer interactions and internal culture.


Key Points • Miller Storm’s mission is to enhance the community by enhancing its people first • Be coachable and open to feedback — ego has no place here • Do what you say you are going to do — your word matters • Commit to daily growth and continuous self-improvement • Always prioritize people over profits

Transcript Welcome to the Miller Storm family. We are a company that holds a very high standard—not only for our customers, but for our sales consultants and everyone inside of this company. Our mission is to enhance our community by enhancing our people first. We are a personal development company, so let’s get into our core values.

Be Coachable Everyone inside this company needs to be open to constructive criticism. We don’t like ego. We don’t like people who come in thinking they know it all, because the truth is we are learning every single day. These values are not just for you—they apply to everyone, including leadership. We all must be able to communicate effectively and properly accept feedback in order to grow.

Do What You Say You Are Going to Do Be impeccable with your word. If you are going to show up at a homeowner’s house at 10:00 AM, be there early. When you say yes, your yes means yes. When you say no, your no means no. If you are unsure, say “might.” But if you commit, you must follow through—on time and exactly as promised.

Continually Evolve We are a personal development company, and we expect everyone to get 1% better every day. Growth is not limited to roofing. It applies to sales, fitness, finance, and every area of your life. We provide consistent opportunities for development, including: • Ongoing internal trainings throughout the week • Programs like Roofing & Solar Reform Alliance • Guest experts such as CPAs, realtors, and investors We do not accept stagnation. If you are not actively improving or setting goals to grow, that goes against our core values.

People Over Profits We operate with a people-first mindset. With homeowners, it is not always the right decision to file an insurance claim. This is not a cookie-cutter industry. We take the time to ask the right questions and do what is truly best for the customer. This also applies internally. We believe in supporting one another. We do not want people who nickel-and-dime teammates for helping with appointments or tasks. We put people first and the profits follow.
