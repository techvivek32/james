## Lesson: Handling Objections
Show: 2

1. When a homeowner says "I'm not interested," the best first move is to:
* Acknowledge it and ask a light question
- Argue why they should be interested

- The conversation is over
* They need more information or trust
- You did something wrong
- You should lower your price
